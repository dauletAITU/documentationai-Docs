## Overview

Calibrating your AI Drone ensures accurate flight and stable hovering.

## Steps

1. Place drone on a level surface
2. Turn on the drone and remote controller
3. Open AI Drone app, navigate to Settings > Calibration
4. Follow on-screen rotation prompts
5. Wait for success confirmation before flying

<Callout kind="tip">
Recalibrate after firmware updates or if you notice unstable flight.
</Callout>
