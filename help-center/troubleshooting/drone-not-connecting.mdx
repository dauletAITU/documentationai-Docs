---
title: Drone Not Connecting
description: Quick fix if AI Drone fails to connect.
---

## Symptoms

- App cannot find the drone
- "Connection failed" error

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Restart devices">
    Power off both the drone and controller, then turn them back on.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Wi-Fi/Bluetooth">
    Ensure your device is on the correct network; re-pair if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update firmware">
    Use the AI Drone app to check for and install updates.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="info">
If issue persists, contact support with your drone serial number.
</Callout>
