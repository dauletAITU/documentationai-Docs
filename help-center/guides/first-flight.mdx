## Overview

Start flying your AI Drone safely in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Charge the battery">
    Plug in your drone and controller until LEDs show green.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect the app">
    Open the AI Drone app and follow connection prompts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Calibrate sensors">
    Go to Settings > Calibration and follow instructions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Take off">
    Move the stick down and out to start propellers, then slowly push up to lift off.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="warning">
Fly in an open area away from people and obstacles.
</Callout>
