## Overview

AI Drone empowers you to organize and manage project documentation effortlessly. You harness AI-powered tools to auto-categorize content, generate summaries, and maintain consistency across your docs. This SaaS platform streamlines workflows, boosts team collaboration, and ensures your documentation stays up-to-date with minimal effort.

## Quick Start

Get up and running with AI Drone in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Visit `https://app.aidrone.com` and create your free account using your email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Workspace" icon="folder-plus">
    Set up a new workspace for your project and invite team members.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Import Docs" icon="upload">
    <CodeGroup tabs="Markdown,HTML">
      ````markdown
      # Project Overview
      Welcome to your AI Drone project.
      ````

      ````html
      <h1>Project Overview</h1>
      <p>Welcome to your AI Drone project.</p>
      ````
    </CodeGroup>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable AI Features" icon="brain">
    Toggle AI organization in workspace settings to auto-generate tags and summaries.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Pro tip: Start with the AI summary generator to quickly outline new documents.
</Callout>
